Manifesto

We are not just customers. We are people who depend on stable, reliable, and transparent services to live, to work, and to survive in a digital age. The Reckoning exists because silence enables decay — and we refuse to be silent any longer.

This platform exists to collect grievances, document failures, and demand accountability. We are tired of the same cycle: outages without answers, bills without rebates, and empty apologies without resolution.

We are not here to shame. We are here to expose. And through collective experience, we push for systemic change. Let this be the archive of what was broken — and the spark for what must be rebuilt.

This is not a threat. It is a reminder: our voices matter.
